INSTRUCTIONS

Follow these steps to optimize your viewing experience:

Follow the Instructions Given by The Coordinator And Ask for your QR code to open the websites

For iPhone:

  1. Set your phone brightness to 100%: Go to Settings -> Display & Brightness -> Brightness and adjust it to the maximum level.
  2. Disable Night Shift: Go to Settings -> Display & Brightness -> Night Shift and turn it off.
  3. Set Auto Lock to "Never": Go to Settings -> Display & Brightness -> Auto-Lock and choose the "Never" option.

For Android:

  1. Set your phone brightness to 100%: Go to Settings -> Display & Brightness -> Brightness and adjust it to the maximum level.
  2. Set Auto Screen Off to 30 minutes: Go to Settings -> Display & Brightness -> Auto Screen Off and choose the "30 minutes" option.
  3. Disable any dark mode, negative tone, blue light filter, or eye comfort settings.
